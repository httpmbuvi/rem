About Us

Who We Are

Rehema Care Services is a dedicated provider of home care, caregiving, and community health support. We offer compassionate assistance for the elderly, sick, and vulnerable members of our community, ensuring they receive the care they deserve in the comfort of their own homes.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, reliable, and professional care services that enhance the quality of life for our clients and their families.

Our Vision

A community where everyone, regardless of age or condition, receives care with dignity, respect, and kindness.

Our Values

  • Compassion
  • Respect
  • Professionalism
  • Trust
  • Community Support
